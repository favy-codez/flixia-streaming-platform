Frequently Asked Questions

What is Flixia?

Flixia is a subscription-based streaming service that allows subscribers watch TV shows and movies without commercials on an internet-connected device.Watch now, cancel anytime.

How much does Flixia cost?

Watch Flixia on your smartphone, tablet, Smart TV, laptop, or streaming device, all for one fixed monthly fee. Plans range from ₦1,200 to ₦4,400 a month. No extra costs, no contracts.

Where can I watch?

Watch anywhere, anytime. Sign in with your Flixia account to watch instantly.

Can I cancel?

You can easily cancel your account online in two clicks. There are no cancellation fees – start or stop your account anytime.
